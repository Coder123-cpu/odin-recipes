French Toast

Description

These French toast slices are golden and crispy on the outside, soft and custard-like on the inside. They are perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Butter for cooking

Steps

  1. Mix milk, eggs, vanilla, sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a shallow dish.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, allowing it to soak for a few seconds.
  3. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook the soaked bread slices until golden brown on both sides.
